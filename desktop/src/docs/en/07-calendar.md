# Chapter 7: The Calendar 📅

The **Calendar** page gives you a traditional calendar view of all your tasks and events. It's the best way to see what's happening on any given day, week, or month.

---

## 🗓️ Calendar Views

The Calendar supports four different views:
*   **Month**: See the entire month at a glance. Tasks appear as colored blocks on their deadline dates.
*   **Week**: Focus on the current week with more detail per day.
*   **Day**: Drill down to a single day to see all tasks and events.
*   **Agenda**: A simple list view of upcoming events, sorted chronologically.

Switch between views using the buttons in the calendar toolbar.

---

## 🧭 Navigation

The top header gives you precise control over navigation:

*   **Month Selector**: A dropdown listing all months (localized in your language). Pick any month to jump directly to it.
*   **Year Selector**: A dropdown with years (current year ± 10). Switch between years instantly.
*   **Today Button** (📅): Click to jump back to today's date immediately.
*   **Refresh Button** (🔄): Reload all calendar data from the server.

---

## 🎨 Color Coding

Tasks on the calendar are color-coded by their status:
*   🔴 **Red**: Overdue tasks — the deadline has passed and they're not done.
*   🟢 **Green**: Done tasks — completed work.
*   🔵 **Blue**: In Progress tasks — currently being worked on.
*   ⬜ **Gray**: Default — tasks in any other status (To Do, Review, etc.).

---

## ➕ Creating Calendar Events

Click on any empty day on the calendar to create a new event:

1. A **"New Event"** dialog appears showing the selected date.
2. Enter a **Title** for the event.
3. Choose the **Type** from the dropdown:
    *   **TASK_BLOCK**: A block of time dedicated to working on something (default).
    *   **MEETING**: A meeting with others.
    *   **CALL**: A phone or video call.
    *   **APPOINTMENT**: A scheduled appointment.
4. Click **"Create"**.

The event will appear on the calendar immediately.

---

## 📋 Viewing Event Details

Click on any event on the calendar to see its details in the **right panel**:
*   **Title**: The event or task name.
*   **Date**: The scheduled date (localized format).
*   **Status Badge**: Current status with color coding.
*   **Priority Badge**: Priority level with color coding.

---

## 📊 Calendar Statistics

The right panel also shows useful statistics:
*   **Tasks with deadline**: Total number of tasks that have a deadline set.
*   **This month**: How many deadlines fall in the current month.
*   **Overdue**: How many tasks are past their deadline.
