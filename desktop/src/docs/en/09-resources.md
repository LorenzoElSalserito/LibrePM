# Chapter 9: Resources & Workload 📊

The **Resources** page gives you a bird's-eye view of how your team's time is allocated. It helps you spot overloaded team members, find available capacity, and balance workloads across your project.

---

## 🗓️ The Resource Grid

The main view is a **grid table** with:
*   **Rows**: One row per team member, with their avatar (initials circle) and name (sticky on the left).
*   **Columns**: One column per day in the selected range, showing the weekday abbreviation and day number.
*   **Cells**: Each cell shows the number of hours allocated to that member on that day.

### Color Coding
The cells are color-coded to instantly show workload levels:
*   🟢 **Green (Optimal)**: Up to 4 hours allocated — healthy workload.
*   🟡 **Yellow (Full)**: 4 to 8 hours — at capacity.
*   🔴 **Red (Overload)**: More than 8 hours — overbooked! Action needed.

A **legend** below the table explains the colors.

Hovering over a cell shows a tooltip with the exact hours estimated.

---

## 🎛️ Controls

The toolbar at the top provides:

*   **Project Selector**: Choose which project's resources to view, or select "All projects".
*   **Start Date**: Pick the first day of the range to display.
*   **Range Duration**: Choose how many days to show:
    *   **7 days**: Focus on the current week.
    *   **14 days**: Two-week sprint view.
    *   **21 days**: Three-week look-ahead.
    *   **30 days**: Full month overview.

---

## 📋 Task Details per Cell

Click on any cell in the grid to see what tasks are assigned to that member on that day:

A **modal** opens showing:
*   The **member name** and the **selected day**.
*   A list of tasks assigned, each showing:
    *   Task title.
    *   Estimated time (or "Not set" if no estimate).
    *   **Remove assignment** button (person icon with X): Unassign this task from this member.
*   If no tasks are assigned, a "No tasks" message appears.

---

## 👤 Member Management

Click on a **member's name** in the left column to open a member management modal:
*   Shows the member's details (ID).
*   **"Remove from Project"** button: Remove this member from the project entirely.
