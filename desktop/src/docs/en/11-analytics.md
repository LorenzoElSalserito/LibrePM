# Chapter 11: Analytics 📈

The **Analytics** page helps you understand your productivity patterns and how accurate your time estimates are.

---

## 🎛️ Project Selector

At the top of the page, a **project selector** dropdown lets you choose:
*   **All projects**: See aggregated analytics across all your work.
*   **Specific project**: Filter to see analytics for one project only.

---

## 🔥 Focus Heatmap

The **Focus Heatmap** widget shows when you're most productive. It visualizes your Focus Timer sessions as a heatmap, revealing patterns in your work habits:
*   Which days of the week you work most.
*   What time of day you're most focused.
*   Trends over time.

This data comes from the Focus Timer you use on Kanban cards (Chapter 3).

---

## 🎯 Estimate Accuracy

The **Estimate Accuracy** widget compares your time estimates with actual time spent:
*   See how well you predicted task duration.
*   Identify patterns of over-estimation or under-estimation.
*   Improve your planning skills over time.
