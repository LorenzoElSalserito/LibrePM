# Change Control & Branches

LibrePM provides formal change management through change requests, project branches, and a decision log.

## Change Requests

Manage project changes formally:
- **Title & Description**: What needs to change
- **Motivation**: Why the change is needed
- **Scope**: Schedule, Budget, Scope, Resources, or All
- **Priority**: Low, Medium, High, Critical
- **Expected Impact**: What effects the change will have

Change requests flow through statuses: Draft, Submitted, In Review, Approved, Rejected, Implemented.

## Project Branches

Create scenario branches to explore alternatives without affecting the main project:
- **SCENARIO** - What-if analysis
- **REFORECAST** - Updated projections
- **CANDIDATURE** - Bid/tender variations
- **PIVOT** - Strategic direction change
- **SPINOFF** - New project derivation
- **EVOLUTIVE** - Incremental improvements

Branches can be linked to change requests and tracked through Active, Approved, Rejected, Merged, or Archived statuses.

## Decision Log

Keep a permanent record of project decisions:
- What was decided
- The rationale behind the decision
- Who made the decision
- Impact summary
- Links to related branches or change requests

This creates an audit trail of why the project evolved the way it did.
