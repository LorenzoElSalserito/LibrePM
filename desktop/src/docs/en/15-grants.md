# Grants & Stakeholders

LibrePM provides tools for managing grant applications, stakeholder relationships, sponsors, donors, and consortium partners.

## Grant Calls

Track funding opportunities through their lifecycle:
- **IDENTIFIED** - Opportunity discovered
- **EVALUATING** - Assessing fit and eligibility
- **PREPARING** - Writing the application
- **SUBMITTED** - Application sent
- **AWARDED** / **REJECTED** - Outcome received
- **CLOSED** - Grant period ended

For each call, you can track the issuer, deadline, available budget, and reference code.

## Eligibility Requirements

Create a checklist of requirements for each call:
- Document requirements
- Eligibility criteria
- Budget constraints
- Partner requirements
- Technical requirements

Mark each as met/unmet to track readiness.

## Obligations & Reporting

- Register contractual, grant, regulatory, and reporting obligations
- Set deadlines and responsible persons
- Track status (Pending, In Progress, Completed, Overdue)
- Define reporting periods with due dates

## Stakeholder Map

The stakeholder register includes an **Influence/Interest Matrix** — a visual scatter plot showing where each stakeholder falls on the influence and interest axes. This helps identify who to:
- **Manage Closely** (high influence, high interest)
- **Keep Satisfied** (high influence, low interest)
- **Keep Informed** (low influence, high interest)
- **Monitor** (low influence, low interest)

## Sponsors & Donors

- Register sponsors with commitments and agreement details
- Track donors and their donations
- Monitor restricted vs unrestricted funding

## Partner Organisations

For consortium projects, manage partner organisations with:
- Country and role (Coordinator, Partner, Associate, Subcontractor)
- Contact details
- Budget share allocation
