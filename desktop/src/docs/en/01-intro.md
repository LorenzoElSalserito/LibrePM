# Welcome to LibrePM! 👋

Hello! Imagine LibrePM as your **digital workshop**. It's a special place where you can organize your ideas, your work, and your projects, without fear of losing anything.

You don't need the internet to use it: LibrePM lives on your computer (it's "Local-First"), just like your personal files. It's fast, secure, and always ready.

---

## 🏗️ Step 1: Who are you? (The Profile)

As soon as you open LibrePM for the first time, it will ask you to create a **Profile**.
Think of the profile as your lab coat. LibrePM needs to know who is moving things around in the workshop.

### How to create your profile:
1. You will see a welcome screen. Click on **"Create Profile"**.
2. **Display Name**: Write your name (e.g., "Lorenzo"). This is what you will see on the screen.
3. **Username**: Write a unique nickname (e.g., "lorenzo.dev"). It serves to distinguish you if you ever work with others.
4. Click on **"Create and Start"**.

🎉 **Done!** Now you are in.

> **💡 Fun Fact:** You can create multiple profiles on the same computer! It's useful if you want to keep work ("Mario Work") separate from hobbies ("Mario Hobby").

## 🧭 Step 2: The Workshop Map (The Interface)

Let's look at the screen together. It is divided into three main parts, like a well-organized workbench.

### 1. The Sidebar (Left)
This is your toolbox. From here you can go anywhere:
*   **Dashboard**: The dashboard with all summaries.
*   **Charter**: Executive dashboard with project charter, OKRs, risks, deliverables and baselines.
*   **Projects**: Where all your work "boxes" are kept.
*   **Gallery**: Pre-configured templates to start new projects quickly.
*   **Team**: Manage team members, assign roles and add collaborators.
*   **Resources**: View workload and resource allocation across your team.
*   **Contacts**: Your professional network — connect with other LibrePM users.
*   **Calendar**: A calendar view of your tasks and events.
*   **Planner**: An ordered list view, perfect for seeing everything together.
*   **Gantt**: A timeline chart to plan tasks with dependencies and milestones.
*   **Kanban**: Cards to move across columns, perfect for seeing where you are.
*   **Notes**: Your notebook with Markdown editor and collaboration features.
*   **Guides**: This documentation — always at hand.
*   **Analytics**: Charts and heatmaps about your productivity.
*   **Integrations**: Calendar feeds, import/export, and data management.

The sidebar can be **collapsed** by clicking the chevron button at the bottom, showing only icons to save space.

### 2. The Top Bar (Top)
Here you find the title of the page where you are.
On the right side you will find several important elements:

*   **Page Actions**: Context-specific buttons that change depending on which page you are on (e.g., "+ New Project", search bars, filters).
*   **🔔 Notification Bell**: A bell icon that shows a red badge with the count of unread notifications. Click it to see:
    *   New comments on your tasks (blue icon).
    *   Incoming connection requests (yellow icon).
    *   Accepted connections (green icon).
    *   A **"Mark all as read"** button at the top.
*   **Panel Toggle**: A button to show or hide the right information panel.
*   **👤 Profile Menu**: Your initials (or avatar) in a circle. Click on it to open a dropdown with:
    *   Your name and username displayed at the top.
    *   **Settings**: Opens the Settings page.
    *   **Info LibrePM**: Shows a popup with version, license, and credits.
    *   **Open Data Folder**: Reveals where LibrePM stores your data on disk.
    *   **Export Database**: Quick export of all your data as a backup.
    *   **Change Profile**: Switch to a different profile (returns to the selection screen).
    *   **Exit**: Close the session (with confirmation).

### 3. The Workspace (Center)
This is where the magic happens! If you are in Projects, you will see your boxes. If you are in Planner, you will see your lists.

### 4. The Right Panel (Right Side)
On the right side of the screen there is an **information panel** that adapts to the page you are on. It can show:
*   Details about the selected item (task, event, member).
*   Contextual statistics and summaries.
*   Helpful tips and explanations.

You can show or hide this panel at any time with the **"Panel"** button in the top bar.

## ⌨️ Step 3: The Command Palette (Power User Shortcut)

LibrePM has a **Command Palette** — a quick-access search bar that lets you navigate anywhere instantly.

### How to open it:
Press **Ctrl+K** (or **Cmd+K** on Mac).

### What you can do:
*   Type the name of any page (e.g., "Kanban", "Notes", "Settings") and press Enter to jump there.
*   Type **"New Project"** to open the new project dialog instantly.
*   Use **arrow keys** to navigate the list and **Enter** to select.
*   Press **Escape** to close.

It's the fastest way to move around LibrePM without touching the mouse!

## 🎨 Step 4: Light and Dark (Themes)

LibrePM supports two visual themes:
*   **Light**: Clean and bright, ideal for daytime work.
*   **Dark**: Easy on the eyes, perfect for late-night sessions.

You can switch between themes in **Settings** → **Appearance** → **Theme**. The change is applied immediately.

---

## 🌍 Step 5: Your Language

LibrePM is available in **Italian** and **English**. You can change the language at any time from **Settings** → **Appearance** → **Language**.

All buttons, labels, and messages will update instantly.
