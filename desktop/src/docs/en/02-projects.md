# Chapter 2: Boxes and Projects 📦

If LibrePM is your workshop, the **Project** is a box where you keep everything needed for a specific job (e.g., "Home Renovation", "School Project", "Summer Marketing").

Instead of having pieces scattered everywhere (and losing them!), each project has its **tasks** (things to do) and its **notes** inside.

---

## 🏗️ Let's Create the First Project!

### 1. Where to go?
Click on **"Projects"** in the menu on the left.

### 2. How to do it?
1. Top right, there is a blue button: **"+ New Project"**. Click it!
2. A small window (a "popup") will appear asking you two things:
    *   **Title**: Write a clear name (e.g., "Company Website").
    *   **Description**: Explain what you need to do (e.g., "Redesign the company website"). It's optional, but it helps to remember.
3. Click on **"Create"**.

🎉 **Hurray!** Your box is ready.

> **💡 Tip:** You can also create a project from the **Command Palette** (Ctrl+K) by typing "New Project"!

---

## 🎨 The Color of Health

LibrePM immediately shows you if a project is going well or badly, coloring the edge of its box. It's like a traffic light!

*   **🟢 Green (OK)**: Everything is fine! No delays.
*   **🟡 Yellow (WARNING)**: Caution! There are some late tasks (1 to 3 overdue).
*   **🔴 Red (CRITICAL)**: Alarm! There are too many late tasks (more than 3 overdue). Immediate intervention needed!

### How to see it?
In the Projects page, each card has a colored bar at the bottom and a colored border if there are problems.

### What else does the card show?
Each project card displays useful information at a glance:
*   **📋 Task progress**: A counter showing completed tasks out of total (e.g., "3/10 Task").
*   **⚠️ Overdue count**: If there are late tasks, a red warning appears (e.g., "2 Late").
*   **📊 Progress bar**: A visual bar showing how much of the project is done.
*   **📁 Archive icon**: If the project is archived, the title appears crossed out with an archive icon.

## 🚪 Open, Close, and Archive

### 1. Open the Project
To enter the box and see all the details, click on the **"Open Project"** button at the bottom of the card. This opens the **Project Detail** window.

#### The Project Detail Window
This is a powerful window with **three tabs**:

*   **📝 Info Tab**: Here you can edit the **Project Name** and **Description**. Click **"Save Changes"** when you're done.
*   **📒 Notes Tab**: View and manage all notes associated with this project directly.
*   **👥 Team Tab**: See a link to the Team management page where you can add or remove members.

At the bottom of the window you'll find:
*   **🗑️ Delete** (red button, on the left): Delete the project permanently.
*   **📦 Archive / Reopen** (gray button, on the left): Archive a finished project or reopen an archived one.
*   **➡️ Go to Planner** (green button, on the right): Jump directly to the Planner view to start working on tasks.

### 2. Edit the Project
Wrong name?
1. In the project list, click on the **three dots** (⋮) top right of the card.
2. Or better: click **"Open Project"**, go to the **Info** tab, change the name or description, and click **"Save Changes"**.

### 3. Quick Actions from the Card
Each project card has a **three dots menu** (⋮) in the top right corner with quick actions:
*   **Archive** (or **Reopen** if already archived): Move the project to the attic without deleting it.
*   **Delete**: Permanently remove the project (with confirmation).

### 4. Archive the Project
Finished the job? Don't throw it away! You can put it in the attic (**Archive**).
1. Click on the three dots (⋮).
2. Choose **"Archive"**.
The project will disappear from the main list, but it's not lost!

### 5. Review old work
Want to see finished projects?
1. At the top of the Projects page, turn on the **"Show archived"** switch.
2. There they are! They are gray to let you know they are closed.
3. If you want to reopen one, click on the three dots and choose **"Reopen"**.

---

## 🗑️ Delete Forever (Warning!)
If you created a project by mistake and want to delete it permanently:
1. Click on the three dots (⋮).
2. Choose **"Delete"**.
3. It will ask for confirmation. If you say "Yes", **it will disappear forever** with all its tasks and notes. Use with caution!

You can also delete from inside the **Project Detail** window using the red **"Delete"** button at the bottom left.
